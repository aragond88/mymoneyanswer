Before you follow money guidance from YouTube

Use videos for education, then verify important details with official sources, calculators, account providers, or a qualified professional who can review your situation.

  • Be careful with guaranteed income or guaranteed investment return claims.
  • Check whether a video is about the United States, Canada, or another country.
  • Look for clear explanations, not only entertainment or urgency.
