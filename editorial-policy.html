Editorial Policy

How MyMoneyAnswer
creates content

The standards used for educational articles, tools, calculators, product mentions, and updates.

Last updated: May 4, 2026

Editorial mission

MyMoneyAnswer creates simple, calm, beginner-friendly personal finance education for users in the United States and Canada.

The goal is to help users understand their options and choose a clearer next step.

Content standards

  • Use plain English.
  • Explain abbreviations before relying on them.
  • Avoid hype, fear-based language, and guaranteed outcome claims.
  • Separate educational information from product promotion.
  • Add clear disclaimers when discussing investing, debt, taxes, affiliate products, or major financial decisions.

Sources and updates

Content should be based on reliable sources, official pages where possible, and clear reasoning. Pages that depend on changing rules, rates, limits, or product details should be reviewed and updated over time.

Product mentions

Products and platforms should be discussed neutrally. MyMoneyAnswer should not rank a product only because it may pay compensation.

Wealthsimple is the only approved near-term affiliate candidate. Any affiliate relationship should be clearly disclosed before or near the relevant link.

Corrections

If you find an error, send the page URL and correction details to hello@mymoneyanswer.com. Corrections should be reviewed and handled as soon as practical.

For general educational information only. Results and examples are not guarantees.
