# Content Rules

## Language

- Public copy must be English.

## Tone

Tone should be:

- Simple.
- Helpful.
- Calm.
- Beginner-friendly.
- Trustworthy.
- Practical.

## Avoid

- Hype.
- Fear-based language.
- Guaranteed results.
- Aggressive affiliate language.
- Personalized advice claims.
- Guaranteed income claims.
- Promised investment returns.

## Disclaimers

Include educational disclaimers on pages about:

- Investing.
- Debt.
- Taxes.
- Retirement accounts.
- Affiliate products.
- Financial decisions.

Use "educational information" or "general information." Do not say "financial advice."

## Clarity

- Never use abbreviations without explaining them first.
- Keep explanations simple and beginner-friendly.
- Define technical terms before relying on them.

## Affiliate Content

- Affiliate content must be transparent.
- Affiliate disclosure must appear before or near affiliate links.
- Content must remain useful even if affiliate links are removed.

## Product Comparisons

- Product comparisons must be neutral.
- Do not rank products only by commission.
- Do not make misleading claims.

## Search Content

- Search content must not be thin or duplicated.
- Each page should have a unique purpose and useful information.
- Do not create pages that only swap dollar amounts, locations, or keywords.
