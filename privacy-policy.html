Some site features may store simple preferences in your browser using localStorage. For example, the site may remember a selected country so the experience can be more relevant to users in the United States or Canada.

The website may link to third-party websites. Their privacy practices are controlled by those websites, not by MyMoneyAnswer. Review their policies before using their services.
