About

Helping beginners make clearer money decisions.

MyMoneyAnswer is a personal finance education website for people in the United States and Canada who want a simple next step, not more confusion.

What MyMoneyAnswer does

MyMoneyAnswer helps beginners decide their next best money move in 5 minutes. The site combines simple tools, calculators, plain-English lessons, and curated resources so users can understand common money decisions more clearly.

The goal is to make personal finance feel practical and approachable. The site is designed to feel like a decision tool, not a generic finance blog.

Who it is for

MyMoneyAnswer is built for beginners in the United States and Canada who are trying to understand topics like emergency funds, debt payoff, investing basics, retirement accounts, and where to learn more.

  • People who want a simple starting point.
  • People comparing saving, debt payoff, and investing priorities.
  • People who want beginner-friendly explanations before making major money decisions.

How the site should be used

MyMoneyAnswer provides general educational information. It does not know your full financial situation, tax circumstances, legal obligations, goals, or risk tolerance.

Use the tools and articles as a starting point for learning. For decisions with major financial, tax, legal, or investment consequences, consider speaking with a qualified professional who can review your specific situation.
